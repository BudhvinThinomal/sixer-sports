History of cricket

Origins of cricket and the early days

The origin of criket as a sport dates to the early 17th century. In Weald south-east engalnd the first known documentation about cricket was found in alocal dictionary where it was defined as a boys game that used bowls .First instances of criket as an oragnized sport with teams of players started in the early 18th century,This century also saw criket being established as a popular sport in england, the First womans criket match was also organized in this century,drafting of the earliest cricket rules and also the begining of the earliest cricket clubs and the opening of the worlds most famous crikcet ground the lords occured in this time period.

A century of rapid development(19th century)

The 19thcentury saw a rapid development of criket as a sport,alothough development laged briefly during the early 19th century due to the lack of invesment, in the latter stages of this century cricket saw a widespred popularity in england mainly due to the invention of rail transport.Cricket also gained international attention due to british troops stationed in places around the globe teaching cricket to the local population where they were stationed.The worlds first international cricket match was held in 1844 between two cricket clubs of USA and Canada.In 1859 worlds first ever overseas tour was held in USA in witch the england team toured the country.And this time period also saw the worlds frist test matches.

The pre Modern era(21st century)

This era was the most intersting time period of criket,this century saw the formation of the Icc(international criket club) then known as the imperial cricket club,also test cricket began to spread worldwide as a popular sport in this era.The begining of Iwcc(International womens cricket council) was also in 1958,and in 1973 the worlds first international cricket match took place.This century also saw the begining of cricket leagues ,the first of them was hosted by kerry parker in 1977.1992 saw the begining of the usage of third umpire and match replays with modern and beautifully designed cricket stadiums.

Modern Cricket

The 21 century saw the begining of cricket as we know it,such as the start of T20 matches. criket also became the second most popular sport in the world mainly due to the advent of live straming criket matches by telivision channels and in september 2007 saw the beginig of the indian premier league, and many technological evoloutions such as infa red cameras and edge detetction technology which led to features as ball tracking,flashing stumps made criket a technologically advanced sport

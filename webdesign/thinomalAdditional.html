Who is Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar

  • Sachin Tendulkar born in 24th April 1973.He is a former Indian cricket player and a former captian for Men In Blue. He is one of the greatest productions for India in all time cricket history. He is the higest runs scorer in all format of the International cricket.
  • Sachin entered to the cricket in age of eleven,He made his International appearance in Test cricket was on 15 November 1989 against Pakistan at age of sixteen. He is the only player to score fifty Test match centuries and one hundreds international centuries. He is the first player to score double century in One Day Internationals. He is hold the record for most runs in both Test and One Day Internationals and the only player to complete more than 30,000 runs in International cricket.

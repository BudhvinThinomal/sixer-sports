    • What is cricket?

  • Cricket is a sport which plays between 2 teams with 11 players in each team. The area has an oval shape with a rectangular 22-yard-long pitch inside the center. In the game process, one team bats and tries to score. While the other team fields and bowls, and attempts to dismiss the batsmen and try to reduse run of the first group. Teams transfer among batting and fielding at the end of innings.
  • Today, it's most popular sport in England, Australia, India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, South Africa, New Zealand, the West Indies and numerous different countries along with Afghanistan, Ireland, Kenya, Scotland, the Netherlands, and Zimbabwe.

    • How cricket is important?

  • Cricket importance is no less than any sporting event. The game also encourages team spirit, helps build up character and brings out the quality of leadership. The game is played between two teams and not between two individuals. The game can be won only if all the members of the team put all their efforts in one direction. Every player has a specific job to fulfill. The game of cricket is also a good specimen of division of work.
  • By playing cricket the grip gets stronger, the eye sight keener, the stamina developed and sturdiness of body and mind increases.
  • The game of cricket involved physical activities for a long period of time. It keeps the players healthy, strong and physically fit. Huge planning is done before every match. The team is selected only after analyzing the strength and weakness of the opponent team. Hence, the game of cricket also promotes mental growth.
  • Although there's some status around, to play cricket you need to be healthy and strong, and have suitable hand-eye coordination and ball-handling skills.
  • Cricket on the whole improves the feeling of co-operation, to work together in harmony. Professional players earn a lot of money by playing cricket. Many good players easily get jobs in offices.
